Featured Projects

At Global Sound, we've had the privilege of providing premium audio solutions for a diverse range of events across Egypt. The following case studies highlight our expertise, versatility, and commitment to excellence in sound reinforcement.

International Business Conference in Cairo

Client: Cairo International Convention Center

Event Details

A three-day international business conference hosting 1,500 delegates from 40 countries, featuring keynote presentations, panel discussions, and breakout sessions across multiple halls.

Challenge

The client required crystal-clear speech intelligibility in the main hall for keynote presentations while simultaneously providing independent sound systems for eight breakout rooms. The main hall's challenging acoustics and the need for multilingual translation systems added complexity to the project.

Solution

For the main hall, we deployed an L-Acoustics KARA line array system with careful optimization to minimize reflections and maximize intelligibility. Each breakout room received a customized Bose RoomMatch system configured specifically for the dimensions and acoustic properties of the space.

We integrated Shure ULX-D digital wireless systems with DIS discussion units for panel sessions, and implemented a comprehensive monitoring system for the translation booths using Yamaha equipment.

Result

The conference organizers reported exceptional sound quality throughout the venue, with many international delegates commenting that it was the best sound they had experienced at a conference in Egypt. The client has since contracted Global Sound for all their major events.

Summer Beats Festival

Client: Egyptian Entertainment Productions

Event Details

A two-day outdoor music festival featuring 15 acts across two stages, with attendance of approximately 8,000 people per day.

Challenge

The festival required two complete stage systems capable of handling diverse musical styles from electronic dance music to acoustic performances. The main stage needed to accommodate international touring acts with specific technical requirements, while the secondary stage needed quick changeovers between performances.

Solution

For the main stage, we provided a full L-Acoustics K2 line array system with KS28 subwoofers in a cardioid configuration to minimize backstage noise. Front-of-house mixing was handled with a Midas PRO X console, while monitor mixing utilized a Yamaha CL5.

The secondary stage featured a JBL VTX system with Crown amplification and a Midas M32 console for both front-of-house and monitor mixing, allowing for rapid changeovers between acts.

Result

All performances were delivered with exceptional sound quality, receiving praise from both artists and attendees. The international headliners' production managers specifically commended the quality of our equipment and the professionalism of our technical team.
